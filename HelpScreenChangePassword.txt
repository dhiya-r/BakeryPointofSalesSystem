Welcome to the Help Screen!

The purpose of the Change Password screen is to allow you to change the password that
you use to login with.

If you are experiencing difficulties please follow the steps below:

Steps to Follow:

1. Enter your old password.

2. Enter your new password.
   -You can view your new password by clicking on the show password field.

3. Confirm your new password by re-entering it.
   -You can view your password by clicking on the show password field.

4. Change your password by clicking on the Ok button.
