Welcome to the Help Screen!

The purpose of the Sales screen is to record sales made. 

If you are experiencing difficulties please read the instructions below:

1) Select the amount that has been sold.

2) Click on a row in the stock table. 
    - It will appear in the stock table.

3) Click on the total button to get the total cost.
   - If you wish to remove any items click on the remove button. 

4) Enter the amount they have given you in the amount field. 

5) Click on record sales to record the sale.

6) Click on Record Stock Sold to record the stock sold.
