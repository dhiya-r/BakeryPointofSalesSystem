Welcome to the Help Screen!

The purpose of the Reports screen is to display different statistics that can be printed.

If you are experiencing difficulties please read the instructions below:

1) To display the daily sales click on the Sales For The Day button.

2) To display a products selling price you must click on the Product Selling Price Button.

3) To display the most expensive items click on the Most Expensive Items button.

4) To display a users daily sales click on the User's Daily Sales button. 

5) If you wish to print any of these then you will display them by clicking on the button 
   and then you will click on print.
